Chapters

The Hells Angels Motorcycle Club is divided into chapters that are typically regionally based (e.g., city, state, country). Each chapter operates relatively independently but adheres to the same overall ethos, traditions, and rules

Positions within a Chapter

*President
*Vice President
*Secretary
*Treasurer
*Sergeant-at-Arms
*Road Captain

Regional and International Hierarchy

The Hells Angels is an international organization, so there is a broader structure that includes regional and international leaders. Higher-ranking members might oversee several chapters in a certain region or even multiple countries.

Specialized Roles/Departments

*While the club doesn't have formal "departments" like a corporation, certain members might specialize in areas like event planning, security, or negotiations with other clubs.
*Members may also be involved in charitable activities or community events, although the primary focus of the club is on its brotherhood and motorcycling lifestyle.

MC (Motorcycle Club) Traditions

* Hells Angels also adhere to the traditional motorcycle club model, which includes rites of passage for new members, including probation periods and initiation rituals.
